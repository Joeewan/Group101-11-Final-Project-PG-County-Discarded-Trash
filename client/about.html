ABOUT WEBSITE

This website displays a visualization of data from LitterTRAK dataset that contains information about yearly data on litter and trash discarded from different regions of Prince George’s County from Prince George’s County OpenData web repository.

HOW TO NAVIGATE

On the homepage, use the search bar to type a specific year within the range to see a visualization of trash data within the inputted year represented in a form of polar/pie/donut chart.
